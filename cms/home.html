Technical Training teaches the skills needed to design, develop, implement, maintain, support or operate a particular technology or related application, product or service. Technical training enables individual to master the technical aspects of their job.

Competitive Training

A Competitive training is an exam used to determine an individual's skill or propensity to succeed in a given activity. Competitive tests assume that individuals have inherent strengths and weaknesses, and have a natural inclination toward success or failure
